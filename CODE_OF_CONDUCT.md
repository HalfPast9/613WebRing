# Code of Conduct

The 613 Webring is a community space for Ottawa's builders, presented by
Builders Collective Ottawa. We want it to be welcoming to everyone — especially
people who are new to coding, open source, or putting their work online.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behaviour

- Be kind, patient and welcoming — assume good intent.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Remember that someone's first pull request might be _this one_.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments of any kind.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Linking sites containing hateful, illegal, or malicious content.

## Site content standards

Sites added to the ring should be your own and should not contain hateful,
harmful, deceptive, or malicious material. Maintainers may decline or remove
entries that don't meet this bar.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may
remove contributions or entries that violate them. To report an issue, open a
private channel with a maintainer or contact Builders Collective Ottawa.
